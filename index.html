Welcome to Knockout Structures.

This is an evolving process that started while cramming to build a complex checkout form for CheapTickets.com Events while working at Ticket Evolution. I have evolved it from the lessons learned on that project.

Why?

I love Knockout JS. It's simplicity and power are what make it so amazing. These extensions are designed to simplify some of the tasks that I have found myself repeating. I am a long time Ruby developer and took inspiration from Active Model, particularly in the ko.Model.validations extension for ko.Model.
